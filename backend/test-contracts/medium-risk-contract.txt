SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Subcontractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on December 15, 2024, between DEF Construction Company ("Contractor") and ABC Electrical Services ("Subcontractor").

SCOPE OF WORK:
Subcontractor shall provide electrical installation services for the commercial office building project located at 456 Business Park Drive.

PAYMENT TERMS:
Contractor agrees to pay Subcontractor within thirty (30) days of completion of work and receipt of proper invoice. Payment terms are net 30 days from invoice date.

LIABILITY:
Subcontractor's liability shall be limited to the contract value. Each party shall be responsible for their own negligent acts.

NOTICE REQUIREMENTS:
Subcontractor must provide written notice within 48 hours of any issues that may affect the project schedule or quality of work.

DELAY PROVISIONS:
In the event of delays caused by circumstances beyond Subcontractor's control, time extensions only will be granted with no compensation for delay. Additional costs due to delays shall be borne by Subcontractor.

TERMINATION:
This Agreement may be terminated by either party for cause upon thirty (30) days written notice. Contractor reserves the right to terminate for convenience with fourteen (14) days written notice.

INSURANCE:
Subcontractor shall maintain general liability insurance with minimum coverage of $1,000,000 per occurrence.

GOVERNING LAW:
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State Name].
