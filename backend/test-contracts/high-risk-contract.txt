SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Subcontractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on December 15, 2024, between ABC General Contractors, Inc. ("Contractor") and XYZ Painting Services, LLC ("Subcontractor").

SCOPE OF WORK:
Subcontractor shall provide all labor, materials, and equipment necessary for interior and exterior painting of the residential development located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, State.

PAYMENT TERMS:
Payment to Subcontractor shall be made pay-when-paid, meaning Contractor shall pay Subcontractor within seven (7) days of receiving payment from Owner. Payment is contingent upon payment from the Owner to Contractor. If Owner fails to pay Contractor for any reason, Contractor shall have no obligation to pay Subcontractor.

LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION:
Subcontractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Contractor from any and all claims, damages, losses, and expenses without limitation. Subcontractor's liability under this Agreement is unlimited and shall extend to any and all damages, regardless of the contract value.

NOTICE REQUIREMENTS:
Subcontractor must provide written notice to Contractor within 24 hours of any issue, problem, or potential delay. Failure to provide such immediate notice shall constitute a breach of this Agreement.

DELAY PROVISIONS:
In the event of any delays to the work, whether caused by weather, material shortages, or other circumstances, Subcontractor shall receive time extensions only with no compensation for delay. No payment shall be made for additional costs incurred due to delays.

TERMINATION:
Contractor may terminate this Agreement for convenience at any time upon written notice to Subcontractor. Upon termination for convenience, Subcontractor shall only be entitled to payment for work satisfactorily completed prior to termination.

GOVERNING LAW:
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State Name].
